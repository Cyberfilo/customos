# Research

This folder holds raw research dumps from exploration phases — for
example, surveys of macOS customization routes (Hammerspoon, Karabiner,
yabai, the Accessibility API, etc.). Material here is intentionally
unpolished and dated; it captures what we learned at a specific point
in time, not what we ultimately decided.

Architectural conclusions drawn from this research live in
`docs/architecture.md` and `docs/decisions/`, not here. If something in
this folder ends up driving a decision, write an ADR for it; the
research note stays as the source material the ADR was based on.
